flotsam --
creating awareness around ocean pollution, one game at a time

-- how to play --

(the game requires a large screen to play, and may take a second to load!)

Flotsam is a word that describes debris or wreckage floating in the oceans. In this game, you're a turtle trying to navigate through plastic flotsam!

Press the left and right arrow keys to move around the screen and dodge the floating objects. Press the green flag to start it.

See how long you can last!
