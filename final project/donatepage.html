see turtles --    
the sea turtle foundation --    
SEE Turtles was launched in 2008 as the world's first effort to protect species of sea turtles through ecotourism. Originally sponsored by the Ocean Foundation and most recently Oceanic Society, SEE Turtles is now an independent non-profit based in Beaverton, Oregon.
Sea Turtle Foundation is a non-profit, non-government group based in Australia working to protect sea turtles through research, education and action. Their misson is to safeguard sea turtle population, migration routes and habitats, and support activities that increase sea turtle numbers worldwide.
sea turtle's 911 --    
the iacpc --   
Sea Turtles 911 continuously advocates for sea turtle rights. They support the advancement of sea turtle conservation by creating a safer oceanic habitat for the continued survival of sea turtles and all marine life, and promotes their peaceful co-existence with humans by ending the global exploitation of sea turtles.
The Inter-American Convention for the Protection and Conservation of Sea Turtles is an intergovernmental treaty which provides the legal framework for countries in the American continent to take action in benefit of turtles. They promote the protection and recovery of sea turtles and their habitats.
